My Vision for Krishnagiri

My vision for Krishnagiri, is a duty with responsibilities and actions designed to promote growth, people's wellness, and prosperity for everyone. Through my strategic approach and steadfast commitment, I will convert challenges into opportunities, paving the way for a brighter tomorrow for our beloved constituency.

  • Upgrade basic amenities such as water supply, sanitation, and electricity to ensure a better quality of life for every resident.
  • Promote skill development programs to equip individuals with the necessary tools for employment and entrepreneurship.
  • Implement preventive healthcare measures and awareness campaigns to promote well-being and disease prevention.
  • Support entrepreneurship initiatives and provide training programs to empower individuals to start their own businesses.
  • Empower women and youth through education, skill development, and entrepreneurship opportunities.
